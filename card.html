Lion

A good gauge of a male lion’s age is the darkness of his mane. The darker the mane, the older the lion.

  • Scientific Name: Panthera Leo
  • Average Length: Males are 1.7-2.5m and Females are 1.4 - 1.8m
  • Average Lifespan: 10 – 14 years in the wild. 20-25 years in captivity.
  • Habitat: Africa (and Asia - see below)

The Lion, known as the King of the Jungle, is the second largest cat in the world. The tiger is slightly larger and closely related. The lion was once found throughout Africa, Asia and Europe but now exists only in Africa with one exception. The last remaining Asiatic lions are found in Sasan-Gir National Park in India.
